Introducing Potholes Detection System

An end-to-end road infrastructure maintenance solution.Our proprietary algorithm uses AI to extract road conditions and street assets.

Welcome to Potholes Detection System(PDS), where innovation meets road safety. In response to the growing challenges posed by road defects, particularly during adverse weather conditions, we proudly introduce our cutting-edge Potholes Detection System. Engineered with precision and powered by deep learning technology.

  • Real-Time Safety Alerts: PDS actively contributes to safer driving with a 93% accuracy, providing real-time alerts about road conditions and bumps, mapping coordinates, and uploading data to the cloud for comprehensive road management.
  • Potholes Mapping: PDS goes beyond detection, mapping pothole coordinates and uploading crucial data to the cloud for comprehensive road management.
  • Community-Backed Innovation: Sponsored by Indian Institute of Technology Indore, PDS is a collaboration between innovation and institutional support. Join us in revolutionizing road safety for a safer tomorrow.

Join us in revolutionizing road safety with technology that not only identifies road defects but actively contributes to creating a safer driving environment. At PDS, we are dedicated to driving innovation for a safer tomorrow.
